"SimpleBeamShot Free"

Thank you for Download.
This asset is simple beam bullets.

Can be fired by placing the BasicBeamShot.prefab or GeroBeamShot.prefab to your project,

From "Beam Param" on the script,
The color in the "Beam Color"
The Speed of Beam in "Animation Spd"
The size of Beam in "Scale"
The flying distance of Beam in "MaxLength"

this asset is done the collision detection of an object other than the "NoBeamHit" and Ignore Raycast" Layer.
